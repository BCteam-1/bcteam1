What is an ERP health check — and does your business need one?

An ERP health check is a structured review of your system to see how well it supports your business today—not just how it was originally configured. Over time, processes shift, data becomes inconsistent, and workarounds creep in. A health check helps uncover these gaps, from outdated workflows to over-customization and reporting issues. It gives businesses a clear view of system performance, improves confidence in data, and ensures your ERP is still aligned with real operations.
